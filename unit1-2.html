Unit 1.2

Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully

Some methods to creating a digital product can include, brainstorming ideas, creating a detailed plan of front and back end development and testing/maintenance of the product. Skills needed would include digital literacy relating to digital technology. Resources would include the use of trello, x-code and slack for file sharing.
