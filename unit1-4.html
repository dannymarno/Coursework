Unit 1.4

What risks might impact a digital project

Digital projects can sometimes fail due to lack of leadership such as nobody wanting to take charge of the direction and creation of the product. Lack of communication within the team and team leaders. Poor planning or lack of product testing by the team can also have an impact on the digital project.
